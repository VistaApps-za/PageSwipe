Step-by-Step

Install Instructions

Choose your browser below for specific instructions.

Google Chrome Recommended
Most Android users - best experience
1
Open pageswipe.tech/app in Chrome
2
Tap the menu icon ⋮ in the top right
It looks like three vertical dots
3
Select "Add to Home screen"
You may need to scroll down in the menu
4
Tap "Add" to confirm
PageSwipe will appear on your home screen
Samsung Internet
Default browser on Samsung devices
1
Open pageswipe.tech/app in Samsung Internet
2
Tap the menu icon (three lines) at the bottom
3
Select "Add page to"
4
Choose "Home screen"
Firefox
Privacy-focused browser
1
Open pageswipe.tech/app in Firefox
2
Tap the menu icon ⋮ in the top or bottom right
3
Select "Install"
Firefox calls it "Install" instead of "Add to Home screen"
Microsoft Edge
Microsoft's Chromium-based browser
1
Open pageswipe.tech/app in Edge
2
Tap the menu icon ⋯ at the bottom center
3
Select "Add to phone"
4
Choose "Add to Home screen"
Brave
Privacy browser with ad blocking
1
Open pageswipe.tech/app in Brave
2
Tap the menu icon ⋮ in the bottom right
3
Select "Add to Home screen"
4
Tap "Add" to confirm

Opera and DuckDuckGo Browser

These browsers don't currently support installing web apps to your home screen. We recommend using Chrome or one of the other browsers listed above for the best experience.

Ready to Install?

Open the PageSwipe app in your browser and follow the steps above.
